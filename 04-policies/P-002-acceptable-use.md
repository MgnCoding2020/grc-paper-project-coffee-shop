**Acceptable Use Policy**
Burn and Churn Coffee

**1.Purpose**
This policy defines acceptable and prohibited uses of Burn and Churn Coffee systems, devices, and network resources to protect business operations, customer information, and company assets.

**2. Scope**
This policy applies to:
  • All employees (Owner, Store Managers, Clerks)
  • Authorized third-party personnel
  • All company-owned devices
  • All Access to company systems and networks
Systems in scope include:
  • Toast POS
  • Google Workspace
  • Company-issued tablets
  • Staff Wi-Fi network
  • Network infrastructure
**3. General Acceptable Use Requirements**
Users shall:
  • Use company systems only for legitimate business purposes.
  • Protect login credentials and maintain confidentiality.
  • Log in only with their individually assigned accounts.
  • Immediately report suspicious activity or potential security incidents to the Store Manager or Business Owner.
**4. Prohibited Activities** 
The following activities are prohibited:
  • Sharing login credentials or using another individual's account.
  • Logging into the Toast POS system using another employee's credentials.
  • Attempting to bypass system security controls.
  • Installed unauthorized applications on company-owned devices.
  • Accessing inappropriate, illegal, or non-work-related content using company-owned devices.
  • Accessing payroll or sensitive business data without authorization.
**5. Company-Owned Devices Use (COBO Devices)**
Company-owned tablets and systems shall:
  • Be used solely for business-related functions.
  • Not be used for personnel social media, streaming services, personnel banking, or non-work-related browsing.
  • Only run pre-approved and pre-installed applications.
  • Remain protected by configured security settings (encryption, passcodes, etc.).
Employees shall not modify the security configurations on company-owned devices.
**6. Bring Your Own Device (BYOD) and Network Use**
Personal devices connected to the staff Wi-Wif network shall:
  • Not be used to access restricted or inappropriate content.
  • Comply with company network usage expectations.
If you want to access non-work-related websites, please use the guest Wi-Fi network. 
**7. Email and Phishing Awareness**
Employees shall: 
  • Exercise caution when interacting with email messages.
  • Do not open suspicious attachments or click suspicious links.
  • Report suspicious emails immediately to the Store Manager or Business Owner. 
**8. Access to Sensitive Information**
Access to payroll and other sensitive administrative information shall be restricted to authorized personnel only (Business Owner and the Store Manager).
Unauthorized access attempts may result in disciplinary action. 
**9. Monitoring and Privacy Notice**
Burn and Churn Coffee reserves the right to monitor the use of company-owned systems and networks to ensure compliance with company policies and to protect business operations.
Users should not expect privacy when using company-owned systems.
**10. Enforcement** 
Violation of this policy may result in disciplinary action, up to and including termination of employment or contractual relationship
