# Vendor Due Diligence Checklist (Draft)

## Purpose

This checklist is used during the vendor risk assessment process to document security due diligence performed on third-party vendors. The review helps determine whether the vendor maintains appropriate security controls to protect company data and systems.

This checklist is typically completed for vendors classified as **Moderate or High Inherent Risk**.
