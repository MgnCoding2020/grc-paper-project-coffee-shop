# Inherent Risk Scoring Worksheet (Draft)

## Purpose

This worksheet is used to estimate the **inherent risk level** of a vendor relationship before reviewing the vendor’s security controls.

The scoring considers factors such as data sensitivity, system access, operational dependency, and regulatory exposure. The resulting risk tier helps determine the depth of vendor due diligence required.

## Risk Tier Guidance

**Low Risk**

Minimal due diligence required. Basic vendor documentation may be sufficient.

**Moderate Risk**

Standard vendor security review should be performed. Security documentation and controls should be evaluated.

**High Risk**

Enhanced vendor risk assessment required. Additional due diligence, documentation review, and risk mitigation planning should be conducted.
