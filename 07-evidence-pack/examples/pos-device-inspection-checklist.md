## Inspection Items (per device)

- [ ] Serial number matches the asset register
- [ ] No signs of tampering on the device casing or seals
- [ ] No unexpected/unknown attachments (skimmers, overlays, extra cables)
- [ ] Card reader slot free of foreign objects or overlays
- [ ] Device located in expected physical position
- [ ] Surroundings free of unauthorized recording devices
- [ ] Firmware/software version matches the approved baseline
