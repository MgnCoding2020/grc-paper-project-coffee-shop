## 3. Checklist

- [ ] Every active account maps to a current employee
- [ ] No shared or generic accounts in use (or exceptions documented)
- [ ] Privileged/admin accounts limited to the minimum necessary (least privilege)
- [ ] Accounts inactive > 30 days flagged for review or disable
- [ ] Terminated/separated employees fully deprovisioned
- [ ] MFA enabled on all accounts that support it (cross-check `artifacts/access/mfa-status.csv`)
