# Stakeholder Interview Questions

Burn and Churn Coffee Security Assessment

---

## Business Operations

- What systems must be available for the store to operate each day?
- What system outage would prevent processing payments?
- What systems do employees use during daily operations?

---

## Identity and Access Management

- How are new employees given access to company systems?
- Are individual accounts used for POS access or shared logins?
- How are employee accounts removed when staff leave?

---

## Vendor Access

- Does the POS vendor have remote access to store systems?
- Who manages POS system updates and maintenance?
- Do vendors store or process customer payment data?

---

## Incident Response

- What would happen if the POS system stopped working during business hours?
- Who would be contacted if suspicious activity occurred on company accounts?
- Has the business experienced phishing or suspicious emails before?

---

## Network Security

- Who manages the store's network equipment?
- Is the guest Wi-Fi network separated from business systems?
- Who has administrative access to the router or firewall?

## Physical Security

- Who, outside the Business Owner, has access to the build, and what kind of locking system is in place?
- Are there currently any surveillance systems on the interior or exterior of the building?
- Are there any deterrents around the build?
