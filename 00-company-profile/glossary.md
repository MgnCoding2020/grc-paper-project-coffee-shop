# Glossary

## Access Control (AC)
The process of ensuring only authorized users can access systems and data.

## Multi-Factor Authentication (MFA)
A security mechanism requiring more than one method of authentication.

## POS (Point of Sale)
The Toast system used to process customer transactions in-store.

## POA&M (Plan of Action & Milestones)
A document used to track security weaknesses and remediation efforts.

## Control
A safeguard or countermeasure implemented to reduce risk.

## Risk
The potential for a threat to exploit a vulnerability.

## Evidence
Documentation or artifacts demonstrating that a control is implemented.

## Least Privilege
The principle of granting users only the access necessary to perform their job.

## Incident Response
The process of identifying, managing, and resolving security incidents.

## Network Segmentation
Separating the guest Wi-Fi network from internal business systems to reduce risk.

## Payment Processor
Third-party service (e.g., Stripe) used to handle card transactions securely.
