# Security Assessment Scope
Burn and Churn Coffee

---

## Assessment Purpose

The purpose of this assessment is to evaluate cybersecurity risks affecting the daily operations of Burn and Churn Coffee.

As a small retail business that processes payment card transactions and relies on cloud-based services, the organization faces several cybersecurity risks including:

- unauthorized access to business systems
- disruption of point-of-sale operations
- compromise of employee accounts
- misuse of the store network

This assessment is intended to identify these risks and support the development of a structured security governance program aligned with recognized security frameworks.

---

## In-Scope Systems

The following systems and services are included in this security assessment:

- Toast POS system and administrative access
- Google Workspace accounts used for business operations
- Store network infrastructure (router, firewall, and Wi-Fi)
- Company tablets used for operational management
- Vendor access to operational systems

These systems represent the primary technology components supporting daily business operations.

---

## Out-of-Scope Systems

The following areas are not included within the scope of this assessment:

- Physical alarm and building security systems
- Supply chain and inventory logistics
- Third-party payment processor infrastructure
- Personal employee devices not connected to company systems

These areas may introduce security considerations, but are outside the current assessment boundary.

---

## Assumptions

Due to the small size of the organization, the following assumptions apply:

- Burn and Churn Coffee does not maintain a dedicated internal IT department.
- Technology services are partially managed by external vendors.
- Store management is responsible for basic user account administration.
- Vendor systems provide built-in security controls that support business operations.

These assumptions will be validated during stakeholder interviews and updated if necessary.

---

## Assessment Objective

The objective of this assessment is to support the development of a cybersecurity governance program that:

- protects customer payment transactions
- safeguards employee and business accounts
- maintains reliable store operations
- reduces operational cybersecurity risk

The findings from this assessment will inform the organization's risk management and control implementation strategy.
