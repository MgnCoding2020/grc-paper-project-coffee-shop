# Data Classification Standard
Burn and Churn Coffee

-----

## 1. Purpose
This document defines how Burn and Churn Coffee classifies business data based on its sensitivity and potential impact if improperly accessed, disclosed, altered, or destroyed. 

Data classification supports the organization's risk management and security program by helping determine appropriate protections for business information.

Proper classification ensures that sensitive information receives stronger protection while allowing less sensitive information to be shared appropriately. 

-------

## 2. Scope
This classification standard applies to all business information created, processed, transmitted, or stored by Burn and Churn Coffee, including data stored within:
  - Toast POS systems
  - Google Workspace
  - Company-issued tablets
  - Internal business documents
  - Administrative systems
  - Vendor platforms that process company data

All employees and authorized vendors who interact with company information must follow this classification standard.

## 3. Data Classification Levels
Burn and Churn Coffee uses the following four classification levels.

| Classification | Description                                                                                                                                                 | Example Data                                                              |
| -------------- | ----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- | ------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| Public         | Information approved for public release with minimal risk to the organization.                                                                              | Marketing materials, store hours, website content                         |
| Internal       | Information intended for internal business use only. Unauthorized disclosure would have minimal impact.                                                     | Store schedules, internal procedures, operational documentation           |
| Confidential   | Sensitive business information requiring restricted access. Unauthorized disclosure could negatively impact business operations or employees.               | Employee contact information, vendor agreements, financial reports        |
| Restricted     | Highly sensitive information requiring strict protection. Unauthorized access could result in financial loss, regulatory issues, or operational disruption. | Payment transaction data, payroll information, administrative credentials |

-----

## Data Handling Expectations
The protection applied to information should align with its classification level.
| Classification | Handling Requirements                                                              |
| -------------- | ---------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| Public         | May be shared publicly upon management approval.                                |
| Internal       | Should only be shared within the organization.                                     |
| Confidential   | Access should be restricted to authorized personnel based on job responsibilities. |
| Restricted     | Access must be strictly limited and protected using strong security controls.      |

Examples of protection mechanisms include:
  - Access controls based on user roles
  - Authentication and password protections
  - Secure storage within approved systems
  - Monitoring and logging of administrative actions

## Data Ownership
Data owners are responsible for ensuring that information is classified appropriately and protected in accordance with this standard.

For Burn and Churn Coffee, the **Business Owner** serves as the primary data owner for business systems and operational data.

Managers may assist in maintaining appropriate data handling practices within store operations.
